Grade-A-Lator

Enter each graded category below (i.e. Exams, Homework, etc.) and the perentage of the final grade they are worth.

Then enter all grades received for each category. Enter 0 for anything you have missed and received a 0 on. Do not include assignments that have yet to be graded.

Press "Do the Math!"









All calculations are just estimates. Remember your professor decides your final grade.
